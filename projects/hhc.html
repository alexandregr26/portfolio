Project Information

Due to the fast expansion of e-commerce, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic, the Hard Hat Cafe hired me as a webmaster to fully implement their first website. The website was required to have a modern and simple design, display the complete menu of the cafe, and have an ordering system for students to select what they wanted and when they wanted it. Since this was to be a quick-access website, being mobile-friendly was also of utmost importance for the management team. The website was completed within two months and I invite you to check it out here:

www.cafe.skule.ca

Website Features

Backend

  • Over 200 items were added to the website and are ready to be ordered anytime.
  • A database was synchronized with the website to determine when a specific product is sold out.
  • WooCommerce was used to implement all e-commerce functionalities and process orders.

Frontend

  • A linear design was created for an easier navigation and better shopping experience.
  • Branding and Contact Information were placed strategically on the website for higher visability.
  • Content was designed to adapt specifically to an user's device to improve the website's mobile-friendliness.
